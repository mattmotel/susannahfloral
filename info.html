Hi, I’m Susannah! 
It’s a pleasure to meet you.

I spent the early years of my career in NYC as a project manager in the fashion industry where I could marry my love of organization and creativity. (Left brain, meet right brain.) But something always felt a little… off.

In 2018 I took the leap and switched things up, taking on an apprentice role at a high-end floral studio in Philadelphia. It’s one of the best decisions I’ve ever made and soon it was easy to see that my background in event planning and design made this new direction a perfect fit.

Working with flowers is an honor, as you have been invited to work with people on some of the most emotional and wonderful days of their lives. First and foremost I take my inspiration from color, letting the hues of the season tell a unique story for each client.

My philosophy is simple: Always be excited to learn, and never stop growing.
